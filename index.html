About Me

Hi! My name is James and I’m an Information Science student at Cornell. I was born in Illinois but grew up in Ithaca, NY. Because of a sibling rivalry with my slightly older brother I wanted to distinguish myself. And so I decided to be creative. I delved into poetry and theatre, and just in general thinking outside the box.

At present day I’m an actor, poet, avid video game player, artist, activist, and web developer. I’m seeking to get involved in cool web design, game design, and software projects as a UX/UI developer and/or project manager.
